Job Title   : Design Professional (Composite)
Domain      : Graphic Design / Visual Design / UI-UX / Brand & Web
Employment  : Full-Time / Freelance
Level       : Entry to Senior

---

ROLE SUMMARY
We are looking for a creative and versatile Design Professional with experience
across graphic design, visual communication, digital and print media, and/or
user experience design. The ideal candidate has a strong design eye, command of
industry-standard design tools, and the ability to deliver compelling visuals
that communicate ideas effectively — whether for print, web, branding, or
digital products.

---

CORE RESPONSIBILITIES

Graphic & Visual Design
- Conceptualize and produce design assets for print and digital channels:
  brochures, posters, flyers, banners, signage, packaging, and newsletters.
- Design logos, wordmarks, icons, and brand identity systems.
- Create marketing and advertising materials aligned with brand guidelines.
- Prepare production-ready files for print (CMYK, 300 dpi) and digital (RGB, 72 dpi).
- Perform image retouching, photo manipulation, and compositing.
- Design four-color layouts and manage print production workflows.

Web & Digital Design
- Design responsive website layouts, landing pages, and email templates.
- Create digital assets for social media campaigns, display ads, and online publications.
- Maintain and update web content through CMS platforms (WordPress, Webflow, Drupal).
- Optimize images and assets for web performance.
- Collaborate with developers to ensure accurate implementation of designs.
- Apply accessibility best practices to digital design outputs.

UI/UX & Product Design
- Conduct user research: interviews, surveys, usability tests, and persona development.
- Create wireframes, user flows, sitemaps, and low-to-high fidelity prototypes.
- Design intuitive, accessible UI screens for web and mobile applications.
- Build and maintain design systems and reusable component libraries.
- Run usability tests and iterate based on user feedback and analytics.
- Collaborate with product managers and engineers to ship polished product experiences.

Brand & Identity Design
- Develop and evolve brand identity systems: color palettes, typography, iconography,
  illustration styles, and photography direction.
- Produce and maintain brand standards guides and style documentation.
- Ensure visual consistency across all brand touchpoints — digital, print, and environmental.
- Lead creative campaigns from concept through final delivery.

Art Direction & Creative Leadership
- Lead creative concepting and ideation for campaigns and projects.
- Supervise and mentor junior designers; review and provide constructive design feedback.
- Manage multiple projects simultaneously, prioritizing workloads to meet deadlines.
- Present and articulate design decisions to clients and stakeholders.
- Conduct competitive visual analysis and stay current with design trends.

---

REQUIRED SKILLS

Design Tools (one or more)
  Adobe Creative Suite (Illustrator, Photoshop, InDesign, Acrobat, After Effects,
  Premiere Pro), Figma, Sketch, Adobe XD, Canva, Quark XPress, Dreamweaver,
  CorelDRAW, Procreate

Print & Production
  Four-color printing, CMYK color management, preflighting artwork, press-ready
  file preparation, die-cutting, packaging layout, large-format print

Web & Digital Skills
  HTML5, CSS3 (basic to intermediate), WordPress, Webflow, email design
  (Mailchimp, Constant Contact), social media asset creation, banner ads

UI/UX & Prototyping
  Figma (components, auto-layout, prototyping), wireframing, user flows,
  usability testing, interaction design, design systems, responsive design,
  mobile-first principles, WCAG accessibility guidelines

Visual Design Fundamentals
  Typography, color theory, layout and grid systems, visual hierarchy,
  composition, illustration, iconography, photography direction,
  image retouching, logo design, brand identity

Motion & Multimedia (nice to have)
  Adobe After Effects, Premiere Pro, Lottie animations, video editing,
  GIF creation, 3D tools (Cinema 4D, Blender)

Collaboration & Project Tools
  Microsoft Office / Office 365, Google Workspace, Slack, Trello, Asana,
  Jira, Basecamp, Dropbox, OneDrive, Acrobat for PDF review and markup

---

QUALIFICATIONS

Education
  - Bachelor's degree in Graphic Design, Visual Communication, Fine Arts,
    Multimedia Design, Web Design, UX Design, or a related creative field.
  - Equivalent professional experience or portfolio-demonstrated skill is accepted.
  - Master's degree (MFA, M.Des) is a plus.

Experience
  - Entry-level: 0–2 years (strong portfolio of academic or freelance work welcome).
  - Mid-level: 2–5 years of professional design experience in-house or at an agency.
  - Senior-level: 5+ years with demonstrated creative leadership and brand ownership.

Portfolio
  - A strong portfolio is required demonstrating range across at least two of:
    print design, brand identity, web/digital design, UI/UX, or illustration.

Certifications & Training (any of the following are valued)
  - Adobe Certified Professional (Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign)
  - Google UX Design Certificate
  - Interaction Design Foundation (IxDF) Certifications
  - HubSpot / Canva / Figma professional certifications

---

SOFT SKILLS
  - Strong creative vision and attention to detail.
  - Excellent visual communication and storytelling ability.
  - Open to feedback; iterative, user-centred mindset.
  - Ability to manage and prioritize multiple projects under tight deadlines.
  - Strong verbal and written communication skills for presenting creative work.
  - Collaborative team player as well as capable of working independently.
  - Passion for design trends, typography, and visual culture.
  - Client-facing experience: ability to interpret creative briefs and manage expectations.
